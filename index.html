Our Services

About Hidden Rock

Founded on the principles of hard work, safety, and precision, Hidden Rock has grown to be a trusted name in excavation and land development across Mauritius. We combine state-of-the-art machinery with decades of collective on-the-ground experience to deliver exceptional results for every client.

Our mission is simple: to provide reliable, efficient, and safe site preparation services. With a large fleet of excavators and 40-ton trucks at our disposal, we are equipped for any task, ensuring we lay the perfect groundwork for your project's success.

Reliable Transport

Our robust fleet is the backbone of our operations. We operate a large number of modern, 40-ton trucks capable of transporting heavy materials, including large boulders, aggregates, and site debris, safely and efficiently across Mauritius.

Whether you need materials delivered to your site or debris cleared away, our transport division is equipped for any task, ensuring your project stays on schedule.
